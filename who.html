A Serene Studio Environment

Located just 30 miles east of downtown Seattle, this picturesque recording studio offers an environment unmatched in today’s industry. Surrounded by the Pacific Northwest’s signature evergreen landscape, The Backwoods is a private getaway as much as it is a workspace.

Live Room

High ceilings, natural light, massive pine logs, and our state of the art acoustic treatment administers a live room nothing short of sonic - a live room that sounds as big as it looks, and looks as good as it sounds.

Accomodations

The Backwoods Recording Studio is committed to catering an at-home experience that is both comfortable and inspiring. Our space features several amenities such as our fully accessible kitchen, private sleeping quarters, flexible group accommodations, multiple lounge areas, runners (upon request), and more.
